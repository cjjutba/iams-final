# Presence Screen Flows

## Faculty Class Detail Flow
1. Open `SCR-022`.
2. Load class-level attendance/presence summary.
3. Drill into student details.

## Faculty Student Detail Flow
1. Open `SCR-023`.
2. Fetch presence logs by attendance context.
3. Display scan timeline and detection status.

## Early Leave Alerts Flow
1. Open `SCR-025`.
2. Fetch early-leave events by schedule/date.
3. Display flagged students with miss counts and detection times.
